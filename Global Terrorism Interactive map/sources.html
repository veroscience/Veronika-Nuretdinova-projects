Global Terrorism Database

The Global Terrorism Database™ (GTD) is an open-source database including information on terrorist events around the world from 1970 through 2020 (with annual updates planned for the future)

Missing entries

  • 1993 data is missing
  • nkill data is missing for 12,527 entries out of 209,706, i.e., ~6%. It's especially high for the period of 1979-1986, for years 1982-1983 it reaches 31%. Therefore, the number of victims for this period is undervalued
  • Latitude and longitude coordinates were added manually for 4,691 entries, based on country data

Other sources

  • Sven Chojnacki, Gregor Reisch – 2008, The Consolidated List of Wars, 1946-2006
  • Center for Systemic Peace, Polity5 Project, Political Regime Characteristics and Transitions, 1800-2018, annual, cross-national, time-series and polity-case formats coding democratic and autocratic "patterns of authority" and regime changes in all independent countries with total population greater than 500,000 in 2018 (167 countries in 2018)
